Sautéed Heart & Liver

Ingredients

  • 200g beef heart, sliced
  • 200g beef liver, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pat the heart and liver slices dry with paper towels. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder if using.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, add the heart slices. Sauté for 2-3 minutes per side until browned.
  4. Add the liver slices to the skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes per side, ensuring not to overcook.
  5. Remove from heat and let rest for 2 minutes before serving.
