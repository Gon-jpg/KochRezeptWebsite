Banana Pancake Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 - 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 3 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Pinch of cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • about 40 gramms of flour (add more if not enough)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs open: into a bowl, mix it for a short time until the yellow- and egg-white are mixed. Add a pinch of cinnamon and a tablespoon of honey. Mix it well together. Add flour and mix carefully again.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a frying pan over medium heat and add olive oil.
  3. Cook the pancake: Once the pan is well heated, you can add a little of the filling you just made into the pan. Cook until the one side has some bubbles, the pancake doesn't seem liquid and doesn't stick to the pan. Flip it over and wait a little bit shorter than you just cooked it.
  4. Take it out: When you cooked it on both sides, you can take the pancake out of the pan and lay it into a plate. You can repeat this process 2 times more. This recipe is mainly made for one person, that serves 3 mediump ancakes.
  5. Add topings: You can add some topics to give the pancake some more flavour. I personally like to add some homemade / sugarfree peanut butter on it.
